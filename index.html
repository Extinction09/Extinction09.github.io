A Pirate's Life



About:
Yar, me hearties! Welcome to A Pirate's Life !
This be an incremental game set in the Golden Age of Piracy. Ye be a pirate, after many-a-months drinking in Tortuga, that has all but 7R left on 'im.


How to Play:
Start yer adventure by pressing the button. Ye were fortunate enough to have saved some reales, or gold, to be able to buy it. Make enough money to buy 5 sloops total, and ye'll have a new ship ready to purchase: The Gunboat. Continue on this path and new ships await your purchase, Cap'n.

Each ship ye purchase gives a certain amount of influence , and ye unlock ships by gaining influence . The ships ye unlock be placed under 2 categories: Merchant and Military . Merchant ships brings in lots of gold, but give little influence . Military ships be quite the opposite, Cap'n - less income but more influence .

Fair winds, Cap'n, and plunder 'til yer scurvy heart's content!
